Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the answers to some of the questions we get asked. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to us directly.

How does Spectrum Meet work?

Our platform utilizes a personalized matching algorithm based on shared interests, sensory preferences, and communication styles to connect autistic children with potential friends who understand and appreciate their unique needs.

Is the platform safe for my child to use?

Yes, safety is our top priority. We implement strict privacy measures and moderation to ensure a secure environment. All users are verified, and interactions are monitored to maintain a positive and supportive community atmosphere.

Can I customize my child's profile?

Absolutely! You can tailor your child's profile to highlight their interests, hobbies, and communication preferences. This helps us find the most compatible matches and ensures that your child's personality shines through.

How does the platform ensure inclusivity and acceptance among users?

We're committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for all users. Our platform promotes acceptance and celebrates neurodiversity, fostering an atmosphere where differences are embraced and respected. We actively encourage empathy, understanding, and kindness among users and provide resources and guidelines to promote positive interactions. Additionally, our moderation team is vigilant in addressing any behavior that goes against our values of inclusivity and acceptance.

How do I get started on the platform?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account, create a profile for your child, and answer a few questions to help us understand their preferences. From there, our algorithm will start matching your child with potential friends, and you can begin exploring connections and arranging playdates.
